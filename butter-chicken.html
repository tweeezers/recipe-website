Butter Chicken

Home

Prep Time: 45 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 500g
  • Butter - 3 tbsp
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken
  2. Cook in butter
  3. Add sauce
  4. Simmer with cream
