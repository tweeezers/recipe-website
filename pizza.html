Pizza

Home

Prep Time: 30-45 mins

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Pizza Base
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Veggies (onion, capsicum, corn)
  • Oregano/Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Spread sauce on base
  2. Add veggies + cheese
  3. Bake at 200°C for 15 mins
  4. Top it with Oregano and Chilli Flakes
