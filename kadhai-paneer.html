Kadhai Paneer

Home

Prep Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Paneer - 250g
  • Capsicum and Onion - sliced
  • Tomato - 2
  • Kadhai Masala- 1tbsp
  • Oil + Salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil → sauté onions & capsicum
  2. Add tomato puree
  3. Add spices + kadhai masala
  4. Add paneer cubes
  5. Cook for 10 mins
