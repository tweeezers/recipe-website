Gulab Jamun

Home

Prep Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Milk powder – 1 cup
  • Flour – 2 tbsp
  • Sugar – 1 cup
  • Cardamom
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Make soft dough
  2. Roll small balls
  3. Fry on low heat
  4. Soak in sugar syrup
