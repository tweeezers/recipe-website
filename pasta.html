Ingredients

  • Pasta – 1 cup
  • Garlic – chopped
  • Cream or sauce
  • Cheese
  • Oregano/Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Sauté garlic in butter
  3. Add sauce/cream and Mix it
  4. Top it with Oregano and Chilli Flakes
